FAQs

If you have a question that you don't see an answer for, please send me a message on Discord!

  • What kind of content is planned for Loverbend?

    • - Main Story and Side Quests
    • - Open Map Exploration
    • - Loverbending Detective Role-Playing Opportunities
    • - Romanceable female and male love interests
    • - Intimate relationships and events
    • - Daily Life Social Simulation with Day/Night Cycles
    • - Mystery Solving Gameplay
    • - Choices that change the story
    • - Visual Novel story progression
    • - Stats and Checks System
    • - Inventory and Currency System
    • - A Dynamic World full of exciting Characters, Cases, and Locations
    • - Secrets for you to discover!

  • How often will Loverbend be updated?

    I aim to update the game every month or two, depending on what scope is required for the next update. My hope is that as Patreon funding increases, I'll be able to release updates faster due to having more time to focus on the game, rather than on putting food on the table.

  • When will the first playable build be released?

    As soon as possible! When I'm not putting food on the table, I'm working on Loverbend. You can follow me on Patreon to be the first to know.

  • How do I know Loverbend will be worth supporting?

    I created Find Love or Die Trying, a romance adventure game rated Overwhelmingly Positive on Steam with 10,000+ Reviews. I also have been doing contract work in the games industry for over 5+ years, so I'm confident I can make this project a success.

  • How can I vote on future content?

    When development is further along, I plan to open up additional tiers on Patreon that let backers vote for what content comes next. Development is not there yet, but I'm working hard to enable this as soon as possible.

  • Who makes Loverbend?

    Auden Cho-Wong is creating the art, story, design, and code for Loverbend. Lascal is helping as an art consultant.

  • Have feedback, or a question that isn't answered here?

    You can join the Discord and chat!
